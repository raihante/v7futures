# Trading Strategy - ICT Style

## Chart Construction Guide

### 1. Identify Consolidation Area

- Find nearest **RESISTANCE** and **SUPPORT** zones
- Draw area between them as consolidation zone

### 2. Potential Breakdown Zone

- Identify area below support where price could drop
- Look for liquidity zones below

### 3. Potential Breakout Zone

- Identify area above resistance where price could surge
- Look for liquidity zones above

## Entry Strategies

### Strategy A: Breakout Entry

- Entry: When price breaks above resistance
- SL: Below 21 EMA (a few ticks)
- Confirmation: Volume spike, candle closes above resistance

### Strategy B: Bounce from Support

- Entry: When price bounces off support
- SL: A few ticks below support
- Confirmation: Bullish candle pattern at support

## Target/Profit Taking

### Fibonacci Extension Method

1. Measure height: Support → Resistance (in consolidation)
2. Project same distance from breakout point
3. Or use next resistance on higher timeframe

## Trend Confirmation

### EMA-200 (200 EMA)

- **Above EMA-200** = Uptrend
- **Below EMA-200** = Downtrend
- Use for trend direction only
